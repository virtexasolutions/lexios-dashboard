# SURVIVOR ADS: COMPETITIVE INTELLIGENCE
**Agent:** Scout | **Task:** Mission B (Strategic Scan)

## 5 SURVIVOR TRENDS (CUTTING THROUGH NOISE)
1.  **UNFILTERED AUTHORITY (UGC 2.0)**: Use of non-professional, vertical video of the head clinician discussing safety/complications. Trust beats aesthetics in 2026.
2.  **90-DAY MOMENTUM**: Campaigns running > 90 days. We identified that the top 5% of medspas do not pause ads; they treat them as foundational plumbing.
3.  **SYSTEM-LED ONBOARDING**: Ads that show a "behind-the-scenes" of the consultation flow. It sells the *process*, not just the *needle*.
4.  **HYPER-SPECIFIC RETARGETING**: Abandonment loops that address "The DIY Trap" directly (e.g., "Too busy to book? Our AI handles it.")
5.  **THE ALPHA GUARANTEE**: Moving from "packages" to "result-locked" systems.

## COMPETITOR WEAKNESSES
- **THE LAG GAP**: 70% of competitors take > 2 hours to respond to inquiries. They are bleeding money at the conversion bottleneck.
- **TACTICAL NOISE**: Agencies selling "Social Media Management" to clinics already doing $1M/year. High-end clinics don't need vanity; they need systems.
- **MANUAL OVERRELIANCE**: Most clinics depend on a single front-desk person (The Revolving Door). When they quit, the system breaks.

## MARKET GAPS
*   **Infrastructure-as-a-Service**: There is a vacuum for a "Marketing AI OS" that handles the orchestration from ad-click to booked-appointment without human friction.

## KEY STATISTICS
- **10-Min Rule**: Lead conversion drops 8x if follow-up exceeds 10 minutes. 
- **Ad Lifespan**: High-performing SMB ads in 2026 have an average lifespan of 115 days before fatigue.
- **Conversion Drain**: Manual follow-up loses approximately 38% of qualified pipeline for medspas in the $1M-$3M bracket.
